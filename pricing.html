Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Free and Pro versions?

The Free version offers basic compression with limited features, while the Pro version provides advanced compression algorithms, batch processing, support for all image formats, larger file sizes, API access, and priority support.

Can I upgrade from Free to Pro later?

Yes, you can upgrade to Pro at any time. Your settings and preferences will be preserved when you upgrade.

Is there a free trial for the Pro version?

Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial for the Pro version with all features enabled. No credit card is required to start the trial.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and in some regions, Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your Pro subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.
